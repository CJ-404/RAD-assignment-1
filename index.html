HELLO THERE ! ! !

Welcome to our rolling dice game. here are game rules.

  1. Each player get a chance to roll dices.
  2. If a player got samescores on both dices ( except 1 ) he can play again.
  3. If a player got 1 for both dices his full score reset to 0 AGAIN.
  4. First player who got 100 scores wins.
  5. There are 3 game level to reach 100 score.
